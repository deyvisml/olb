Apple, Google or Microsoft accounts

Do you have an Apple, Google or Microsoft account?

You can use your Apple, Google or Microsoft account to create a new Oxford Learner's Bookshelf account.

This is not available on Linux.

Note: you cannot use your Apple, Google or Microsoft account to create a new account if you have used it for another Oxford ID.

To do this:

1.Choose Register.

2.Choose Continue with Apple, Continue with Google or Continue with Microsoft.

3.Sign in to your Apple, Google or Microsoft account.

Note: If you register with Apple, you have the option to hide your email address. Emails from us will be forwarded to your Apple email address.
 
Remember not to turn off email forwarding on your Apple account. If you turn off email forwarding on your Apple account, you won’t receive emails from us.

Do you have an Oxford ID?

You can add your Apple, Google or Microsoft account to your Oxford ID.

How do I find my Oxford ID password if I’ve registered with Apple, Google or Microsoft?

You may need to know your Oxford ID password to

•access another OUP website that does not yet support signing in with Apple, Google or Microsoft

•add another Apple, Google or Microsoft account to your Oxford ID

•change your username

•remove your Apple, Google or Microsoft account but keep your username and password

To find your Oxford ID password, choose Forgotten your password? when you are asked for your password.
