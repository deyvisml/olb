Reading diary

Your reading progress for Graded Readers is saved to your Reading diary. To see your progress, click the Reading diary tab.

Note:
You won't see any reading progress for your use of coursebooks. Only Graded Readers are shown in the Reading diary.
You'll need to be connected to the internet to update your Reading diary.

To share information from your Reading diary with your parent or teacher, see Reading certificates.

Please choose an option:

•Reading awards

•Number of words read

•Time spent reading

•Books I have read

•Books I am reading

Reading awards

You'll receive an award for each Graded Reader you finish reading. You can see your awards as badges (shown above) or stars. Click the arrows on either side of the awards section to change whether you see badges or stars.

Number of words read

Here you'll see the number of words you've read in all of your finished books.

Note:
Only words from finished books are shown here. The number will update each time you finish a book.

Time spent reading

Here you'll see the total time you've spent reading Graded Readers. The time shown includes books you're reading and books you've finished.

Books I have read

Here you'll see the books you've finished. The information below is available for each book:

•The title of the book

•The CEFR level of the book

•The number of words in the book

•The time you spent reading the book

•The date you last read the book

Books I am reading

Here you can see the books you've started reading, and how much of each book you've read so far.

To continue reading a book, go to your Bookshelf, find the book and click the open book icon.

You can hide books from this section if you want to. To do this, click the Hide button under the book cover, then click OK. The book will be hidden until you start reading it again.
