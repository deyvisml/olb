See your progress

If your coursebooks can share information with Oxford English Hub, you can see your scores and activity data using Oxford English Hub.

How do I know if my coursebook connects with Oxford English Hub?
If you see a Focus option in the toolbar, your coursebook can share progress information with Oxford English Hub.

See your progress on Oxford English Hub

Note:
You do not need to be in an organization to see your progress in Oxford English Hub.

To see your progress through course materials, choose the My progress button on the Oxford Learner's Bookshelf homepage. You will see information about your scores and activity in Oxford English Hub.

You can also choose Go to Oxford English Hub in your account details.
