Download books

If you are using a shared computer, see also Using e-books offline on a shared computer.

To download a book:

1.Click the Download icon for the book you'd like to download:

2.Click the Download button:

The book will download to your computer so that you can use it offline.

Note:
Books can take several minutes to download, depending on the file size and the speed of your internet connection. Books that are downloading will show a progress bar.
You can't start using a book until it has downloaded and installed on your computer.

Problems downloading

If the download stops, you'll see a “Stopped” message. To continue downloading:

1.Click Stopped.

2.Click Download to resume the download.

If you see a message that you are not connected to the internet, check your connection, then try downloading the book again.
