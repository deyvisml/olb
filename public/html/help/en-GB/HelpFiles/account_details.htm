Change your information

Note:
You'll need to be connected to the internet to change your account details.

To change your Oxford ID username or password:

1.Click your initials in the top right corner of the screen and choose Edit under My profile.

2.Choose Change my username or password?

3.Enter your current password and choose Continue.

4.Make your changes.

5.Choose Save.

Note:
Updating your Oxford ID username or password will change these details for any Oxford University Press websites or apps you use with your Oxford ID.
