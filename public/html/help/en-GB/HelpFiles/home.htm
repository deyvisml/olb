Note:
This website is for use on computers and laptops.
To use Oxford Learner's Bookshelf on a tablet, download the app for iPad or Android.

 

All you need to register is an email address. You can also register with an Apple, Google or Microsoft account.

 

Creating your Oxford ID will allow you to access all your books, notes and answers from any supported device.

Are you registered with:

•Oxford Learn

•Oxford Learner's Dictionaries

•Oxford Test of English

•Oxford Teachers’ Club

•Oxford Online Practice, such as Metro Online Homework

If you use any of these websites or apps, you already have an Oxford ID. You don't need to register again. Instead, sign in with your Oxford ID username and password.

 

To create your Oxford ID:

1.Choose Register in the top right corner of the screen.

2.Enter your details. Your username must be a valid email address.

3.Tick the box to accept our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.

4.Choose Create my account.

5.Choose Start using Oxford Learner's Bookshelf.

6.Enter your Oxford ID username and password to sign in.
