Update your books

Sometimes, we update our e-books to add new functionality or fix problems. When we update an e-book, you'll need to download a new copy to make use of the new features. Your notes, answers and changes will all be saved.

When there is an update available for an e-book, a bell icon will appear on the book cover.

To update an e-book:

1.Select the bell icon on the book you'd like to update.

2.Select Update now. You can also update later by selecting I'll do it later.

The updated version of your e-book will start downloading automatically.

 

Note:
Books can take several minutes to download, depending on the file size and the speed of your internet connection. Books that are downloading will show a progress bar. You cannot start using a book until it has downloaded and installed on your computer.
