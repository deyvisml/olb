Sign in

To sign in, click Sign in and enter your Oxford ID username and password.

Note:
You'll need to be connected to the internet to sign in. You usually only need to sign in when you start using the app, or after a large update.

You can also add an Apple, Google or Microsoft account to your Oxford ID so you can sign in with Apple, Google or Microsoft.

If you have forgotten your password:

1.Choose Forgotten your username or password? under the password box.

2.Enter your username.

3.Choose Continue.

You'll be emailed a link to set a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your junk or spam folder.

Forgotten your username? Email Customer Support at eltsupport@oup.com, including any access codes you have used.

Note:
You don't need to sign out when you have finished using the Oxford Learner's Bookshelf. If you are using a shared computer, your Oxford ID will be linked to your user profile on your computer. This means that no one else can access your e-books. See Using e-books offline on a shared computer for more information.
